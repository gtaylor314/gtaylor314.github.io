My Story

Hello! Welcome to my personal website! My name is Garrett and I am so excited that you are here!

To share a little about myself, just about a year ago now, I left my job in the Information Technology field to pursue my dream of becoming a software developer. I had a bachelor's degree in computer engineering, I enjoyed writing code, and I longed for a job where I could stretch my creative muscles, attack interesting problems, and work hard at work worth doing. So I took the leap of faith and I am so glad I did!

I have spent the past year taking online development courses, reading through copious amounts of documentation, updating, practicing, and bettering my skill set and, now, I'm looking to start my career. Feel free to explore some of my more fun projects and more of my experience down below. Thanks!

Where I Am and Where I've Been

07/2022-Present
Career Transition

I "burned my ship," as it were, and left my job at the Manatee County Sheriff's Office. I didn't want to give myself an easy way out when the pursuit of my dream career became difficult. Currently, I spend my time taking online courses, reading through volumes of documentation, learning new skills, updating old skills, and gaining as much experience as I can in pursuit of a career in software development.

08/2020-07/2022
Radio/Mobile Device Analyst

My final role at the Manatee County Sheriff's Office. As a newly created position, I had to break a lot of new ground. I learned a great deal about project planning, product roll outs, running product demos, and soliciting feedback from often non-technical staff.

08/2017-08/2020
Network Analyst

My second role at the Manatee County Sheriff's Office. Having had no prior training, I took numerous courses, obtained certifications from VMware, Dell, NetMotion, and Microsoft, and established relationships with fellow industry analysts. I learned to dig through documentation, plan and document maintenance procedures, and to work with industry partners to make maintenance and upgrade impacts minimal.

04/2016-08/2017
Service Desk Technician

My first role at the Manatee County Sheriff's Office, and my first experience with providing enterprise level support. I learned how to provide effective remote support, how to work with others on separate shifts and rotations, how to document issues and solutions for others to use, and how to provide on-call support while wiping the sleep from my eyes.

05/2014-04/2016
Desktop Support Technician

My first job. Support was all face-to-face at Icard Merrill and rarely over the phone; often with someone watching over your shoulder waiting for you to be done. I learned to work with distractions, to work alongside people of varying demeanors, to adapt to the individual's needs, and to ask for help when I needed it.

12/2013
Graduate of the University of South Florida

I earned a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering with a GPA of 3.86; graduated magna cum laude. I became a member of the Order of Engineer and my senior project, an assistive Android app to control a wheelchair-mounted robotic arm, earned my team mates and I a patent.
